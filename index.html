Trump's Tangents

On April 26, 2018, the hosts of Fox & Friends interviewed President Trump. A few minutes into the call, Steve Doocy discusses James Comey and the memos, asking Trump to comment:

Your opinion on what Mr. Comey is doing on his book tour and the fact that he had a special friend at Columbia University with an FBI badge.

President Trump starts answering the question, discussing Comey for 33 seconds, but quickly moves on to other topics. I thought it would be neat to visualize how Trump moves from topic to topic, and how much time he spends on a given topic. How many Trump tangents can happen in 4 minutes and 27 seconds?
